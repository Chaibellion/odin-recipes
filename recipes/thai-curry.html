Thai Curry

Description

Its Thai Curry, please do not give me shit for the fact this is not traditional. These recipes are from my mother for a 19 year old kid who has moved out and struggles to feed herself.

Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

  • Jar of red/green/yellow curry paste
  • Rice or rice noodles
  • Meat/fish cubed - 100g per person
  • Tin baby corn - hung, drained, and quarted.
  • 1 Cup frozen green beans, or 1 capsicum sliced.
  • 1 Sliced onion
  • 1 Can coconut milk or cream.
  • 1 Dessert spoon cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Boil kettle for cooking rice, heat oil in pan.
  2. When oil is hot, add 1 heaped DSP curry past and stir.
  3. Put rice onto boil.
  4. Add onions, cook 30 seconds.
  5. Add coconut milk/cream, stir
  6. Add veges, cook 5-7 minutes, stir occasionally.
  7. Add meat/fish, cook 5 mins max.
  8. I'm realizing here that my mother tells me to cook the meat for 5 minutes, which would leave it still raw. So please cook the meat/fish first.
  9. Drain rice, pour over boiling water
  10. Enjoy!
