Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese, reheat it easily. Tastes good, put it on other things if you don't have spaghetti.

Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

  • 400g Beef mince
  • 1 Onion
  • 4-6 Mushrooms
  • 2 cloves crushed, or 2 teaspoons minced garlic from jar. (Add more)
  • 1 Can of tomatoes, crushed.
  • Beef stock cube.
  • Salt, pepper, mixed herbs, your choice.
  • Pasta
  • 1 Dessert Spoon of Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil, brown the mince. Add onion and garlic.
  2. Boil jug to cook pasta.
  3. Once water from mince has cooked off, add tin of tomatoes
  4. Add 500ml beef stock (boiled water, stock cube, make it in a jug, stir, add any herbs)
  5. Cook pasta
  6. Add any other veges. can add extra vege to bulk it out.
  7. Stir as needed, cook until its thickened nicely.

*To make this into a chilli/nachos, swap the mushrooms for chilli beans or red kidney beans and add chilli powder instead of herbs.
